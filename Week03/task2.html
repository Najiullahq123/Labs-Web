A "Smart Wheat Farming System" is an advanced agricultural solution leveraging technology to optimize wheat cultivation. It integrates various smart technologies such as IoT sensors, data analytics, and automation to monitor and manage wheat crops efficiently. Additionally, it may incorporate predictive analytics to anticipate potential crop diseases or pest infestations, allowing farmers to take proactive measures for crop protection. Overall, the Smart Wheat Farming System aims to enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability in wheat farming through intelligent data-driven decision-making.
